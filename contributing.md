# Contributing

Contributions are welcome — especially from researchers who have used AI in their own computational work and want to share what they learned.

## What we're looking for

- **Case studies** from real projects. What worked, what failed, what you learned.
- **Corrections** to existing content. If something is wrong or outdated, please say so.
- **New lessons** that aren't covered. Different domains, different tools, different failure modes.

## Requirements

- Everything must come from **real, tested experience**. No theoretical frameworks or untested advice.
- **Document what went wrong**, not just what worked.
- **Pin your versions** — which AI model, which software, which environment.
- **Don't overclaim.** If something only worked sometimes, say so.

## How to contribute

Open an issue or pull request. For small corrections, a PR is fine. For new case studies or guides, open an issue first to discuss scope.
