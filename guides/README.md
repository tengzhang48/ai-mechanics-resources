# Guides

## Current guide

[**Human–AI Research in the Agent Era: From Division of Labor to Division of
Authority**](lessons_human_ai_research_agent_era.md) is the current July 2026
methodology. It covers research taste, agent mathematical and computational
contributions, verified discovery without a known final answer, verification
versus validation, multi-agent review by premise and failure mode,
evidence-governed trust, question formulation, and an operating model for
autonomous research agents. The 24 July 2026 update adds the matching-circle
observable-design example, the distinction between same-cell restriction and
independent local refinement, an observed wrong-boundary-value-problem case,
challenge dispositions, parallel-execution provenance, and artifact-specific
submission closure.
